# Hi, I’m Shubham Kumar

I’m a **Senior Data Scientist** who uses deep learning to solve computer-vision problems. My work spans representation learning, detection, segmentation, classification, and generative modeling—from studying the underlying ideas to training and deploying systems on real data.

I’m especially interested in the gap between why a method should work and what it takes to make it work in practice. That keeps me moving between theory, experimentation, optimization, and debugging. This site is where I share the ideas and practical lessons I find useful along the way.

## Selected work

### Large Vessel Occlusion detection

Deep-learning-based LVO detection on CT angiography, resulting in an ICCV CVAMD publication and a granted US patent, and presented at WSC 2022.

[Paper](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2023W/CVAMD/html/Kumar_Mind_the_Clot_Automated_LVO_Detection_on_CTA_Using_Deep_ICCVW_2023_paper.html) · [Patent](https://www.freepatentsonline.com/11967079.html) · [WSC presentation](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/full/10.1177/17474930221125973)

### Pulmonary-nodule analysis

Detection, segmentation, and longitudinal tracking of pulmonary nodules across follow-up chest CT scans for progression monitoring.

[UKIO presentation](/assets/files/UKIO-Pulmonary-Nodule-Tracking-Presentation.pdf)

### Cardiac MRI generation and analysis

Cardiac MRI synthesis with anatomically conditioned latent diffusion, alongside representation-learning methods for cardiac view classification.
